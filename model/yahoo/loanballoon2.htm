What is a Balloon Payment Loan?

This loan option is often used in purchases as an alternative to conventional lending. It is a regular loan with a balloon payment or future payment due at the end of the term of the loan. This type of loan is used most frequently for autos and real estate. It allows you to make your purchase while reducing your monthly payment and postponing a large amount of the principal to the end of the contract. The advantages are not only the lower monthly payment but a large reduction in interest expense. Even though the payments may not seem to be significantly lower, the amount of the principal going toward paying off the loan is proportionately much greater.

The Balloon payment loan can be used for:
1. Auto purchases
2. First time home buyers
3. No money down real estate purchases
4. Commercial property
5. Personal loans

Example of a balloon payment Loan for autos - this type of loan is offered by many credit unions and auto dealers - Minimized Auto Payment:

This loan option is often used in auto purchases as an alternative to leasing. It is called MAP which stands for Minimized Auto Payment loan. It is a regular vehicle loan with a balloon payment due at the end of the term of the loan. It allows you to own a new vehicle with a used vehicle payment. All new and used vehicles up to 2 model years old are eligible for MAP. New vehicles can be financed for 12-60 months. Used vehicles may be financed 12-48 months depending on their age. There are no penalties for pre-payment.

The future value, or balloon amount, will be determined prior to loan closing and be guaranteed.

At the end of the MAP loan term, the choice is yourssbn@financialcalculators.com
1. Return the vehicle to your credit union or dealer.
2. Sell or trade the vehicle and pay off the remaining loan balance.
3. Keep the car and refinance the remainder of the loan.

Benefits of Loaning versus leasing:
1. Owning a new, warranted vehicle for the lowest possible payment.
2. No security deposits.
3. No "wear and tear" penalties.
4. Easy-to-understand documents - no fine print or hidden clauses
5. No end-of-lease surprises
