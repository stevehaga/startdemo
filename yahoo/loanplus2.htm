Power of the Extra Payment

Many times we have considered the best way to pay off our debts. Should we just borrow the money and pay off the debts with a lower interest rate loan? Should we refinance? Should we apply additional payments to the loan? Which of these is the most frugal use of our cash flow?

In today's financial market place loans often carry very high interest charges. This is normal especially when purchasing equipment or when good credit has not been established.

By making extra payments you have 3 major benefits:
1. You reduce the outstanding balance of the loan.
2. Reduce the length of time left to pay.

But the most significant is:
3. You receive immediate guaranteed return on your extra payment equal to approximately 1/2 of the interest rate you are paying on the loan. This is realized in the next month's reduction of the interest due.
